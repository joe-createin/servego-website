FAQ

Is ServeGo free for users?

Yes. Browsing, booking, and messaging are free for users. Some services may have provider-set fees.

How do I become a provider?

Download the ServeGo Provider app, create your profile, choose categories, and submit basic verification. You'll start receiving nearby jobs after approval.

Which locations do you support?

We're launching city by city. If we're not in your area yet, follow @servegoofficial for updates.

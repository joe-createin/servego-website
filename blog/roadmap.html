Roadmap: What’s Next

At ServeGo, launch is just the beginning. We’re building step-by-step with the community — listening to users and providers to shape the future. Here’s a peek at what’s coming.

1) Payments (secure & local)

We’re integrating trusted providers per region (Stripe/Apple Pay/Google Pay for US; MoMo/ZaloPay for VN). Expect secure in-app checkout, clear receipts, and a wallet where providers can track payouts.

  • In-app pay with cards and local methods
  • Escrow-style flow for safer transactions
  • Provider wallet + payout scheduling

2) Category expansion

Beyond our core services, we’re adding more depth: home services, beauty, tutoring, wellness, and professional categories. We’ll group them into clean themes so users discover faster.

3) Ratings & Reviews 2.0

Reviews will be richer and more helpful: verified-only ratings, dimensions like punctuality/communication, and reputation signals that reward consistency.

4) Smarter search & matching

We’re improving relevance: better handling of typos and local terms, plus matching that considers distance, availability, rating, and response time.

5) Better notifications

Push notifications (post-approval), vibration feedback, and helpful reminders like “Your provider is on the way.”

6) Provider tools

  • Advanced scheduling (calendar view, time-off)
  • Earnings insights (daily/weekly/monthly)
  • Multi-service profiles (offer multiple categories)

7) Community & safety

Stronger in-app reporting, periodic re-verification, fraud detection, and category restrictions if needed — to keep the marketplace fair and accountable.

Building with you

We’ll roll out updates in stages and share progress publicly. Your feedback shapes our priorities — tell us what you need most, and we’ll build it together.

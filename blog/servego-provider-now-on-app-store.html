🚀 ServeGo Provider App is Now on the App Store

We’re excited to announce that the ServeGo Provider app is officially available on the Apple App Store !

Providers can now download the app, create their profile, select service categories, and start receiving nearby jobs right away. With ServeGo, you can manage schedules, chat with customers, and track earnings — all in one simple app.

  • 📅 Manage your availability and bookings
  • 💬 Secure in-app messaging

  • 💵 Track daily and weekly earnings
